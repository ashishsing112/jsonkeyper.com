Privacy Policy

Welcome to JSON Keyper. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We are committed to maintaining transparency regarding how your information is handled.

No Data Storage

JSON Keyper does not store any user data. Any JSON you paste on our website is processed entirely in your own browser. This means that we do not collect, store, or share your data. Your privacy is completely protected while using our tool.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements when you visit our website. Google AdSense may use cookies to provide relevant ads based on your visits to this and other websites. However, JSON Keyper itself does not collect any personally identifiable information.

Cookies

JSON Keyper does not use any cookies for tracking purposes. However, third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites on the internet.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing them with your personal information.
